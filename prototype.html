Overview of the Application Prototype

An application prototype is a preliminary version of the EduConnect app that allows stakeholders to visualize and interact with its key features. It plays a crucial role in the app development process, providing insights into the app's functionality and design.

Benefits of Prototyping

Prototyping offers several advantages in the app development process:

  • Visualize App Functionality: Stakeholders can see and interact with the app before development.
  • Collect Feedback Early: Users and stakeholders can provide valuable feedback during the prototype stage.
  • Refine Design and Features: Iterative prototyping allows for continuous improvement and refinement.
